## Brand Standards Summary

**DO:**
- Emphasize AI and automation capabilities
- Lead with results and metrics
- Focus on personalized, relationship-based approach
- Highlight proven track record and client success
- Use professional, confident tone
- Include clear next steps and CTAs

**DON'T:**
- Generic marketing speak without specifics
- Overlook the technology/AI differentiator
- Minimize the personalized service approach
- Forget to include social proof and testimonials
- Use overly technical jargon without context
